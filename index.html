Visual Neuroscience Lab


We are the Visual Neuroscience Lab within the Cognitive Psychology group at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. Our lab is also based at the Spinoza Centre for Neuroimaging of the Royal Dutch Academy of Sciences.

We perform imaging and eye movement experiments to investigate how the visual brain represents information, and how its information processing changes under influence of cognitive factors such as attention and reward. For more information on the topics we research, please see the science section.
