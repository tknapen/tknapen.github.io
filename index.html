Visual Neuroscience Lab

Understanding in the visual brain

We are the Visual Neuroscience Lab within the Cognitive Psychology group at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. We perform imaging and eye movement experiments to investigate how the visual brain represents information, and how this changes under influence of cognitive factors such as attention and reward.
